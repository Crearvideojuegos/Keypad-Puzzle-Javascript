Keypad puzzle

Rules

-To win you have to turn on all the lights on the keyboard.

-Pressing a button will turn on or off the adjacent button lights both horizontally and vertically.

-For example, if you press 2, the lights in positions 1, 2, 3 and 5 will change. If you press 5, the lights in positions 2, 4, 5, 6 and 8 will change.

You have opened the door!
